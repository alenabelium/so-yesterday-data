Apple's appointment of a hardware-focused CEO signals a major industry shift away from expensive cloud-based AI toward on-device inference, offering fixed costs and enhanced privacy. OpenAI's new Workspace Agents represent a transition from simple chatbots to autonomous automation layers that can coordinate across multiple tools and execute scheduled tasks directly within existing platforms like Slack.

## 2. New SaaS Pricing Models for Autonomous Agents

Major vendors like Salesforce, Microsoft, and ServiceNow are introducing a 'second meter' billing layer that charges for autonomous agent actions and work units rather than just per-seat licenses. This structural change in procurement requires businesses to negotiate clear caps and distinguish between read, write, and execute actions to avoid hidden costs as agents become mission-critical.

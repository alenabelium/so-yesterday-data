## 1. Shift to 'Knowledge Layers' and Structured Retrieval for AI Agents

Major infrastructure vendors are moving beyond vector search to specialized 'Knowledge Layers' that handle structured data like tables and graphs, addressing the 'rediscovery problem' where agents waste compute re-fetching context. This shift requires developers to define precise retrieval contracts to ensure agents receive authoritative, structured context rather than just semantically similar text.

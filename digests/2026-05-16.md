Major infrastructure vendors like Pinecone and Microsoft are moving beyond generic vector search to specialized 'Knowledge Layers' that handle structured data like tables and graphs. This shift addresses the 'rediscovery problem' where agents waste compute re-fetching context, establishing a new standard for building reliable, enterprise-grade AI agents that require precise retrieval contracts rather than just semantic similarity.

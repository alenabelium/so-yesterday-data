## 3. The 'Karpathy Loop' Enables Local Hard Takeoff for Small Teams

A new optimization pattern called the 'Karpathy loop' allows small teams to achieve asymmetric competitive advantages by using AI agents to iteratively optimize their own agent harnesses and system prompts against specific metrics. This 'local hard takeoff' creates a scenario where agile 3-5 person teams can outpace large enterprises that lack the necessary eval infrastructure and governance to run these rapid, automated improvement cycles.

## 4. Portable 'Bring Your Own Context' (BYOC) Strategy Emerges for Worker Mobility

The emerging solution is a 'Bring Your Own Context' strategy using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to extract personal domain knowledge and behavioral patterns into portable, user-controlled databases, ensuring productivity follows the worker.

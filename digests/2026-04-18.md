## 5. Post-Labor Economic Frameworks and the Deflationary Death Spiral

As AI and robotics decouple wages from GDP, experts are proposing a transition to post-labor economics where household income shifts from traditional employment to government transfers and capital ownership. This strategic pivot addresses the 'deflationary death spiral' by utilizing mechanisms like sovereign wealth funds and employee-owned trusts to sustain aggregate demand in an automated economy.

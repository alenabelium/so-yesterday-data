Experts warn that workers are losing critical productivity assets when switching jobs because their AI context remains trapped in proprietary platforms. The solution is a 'Bring Your Own Context' strategy using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to extract tacit knowledge into portable, user-controlled databases, ensuring that personalized AI intelligence travels with the professional.

## 5. Post-Labor Economics: The Deflationary Spiral and the Shift to Capital-Based Income

As AI automation accelerates, the 'AI layoff trap' creates a deflationary spiral where wages decouple from GDP, necessitating a transition to post-labor economics. The proposed solution involves shifting household income sources from wages to capital assets, sovereign wealth funds, and government transfers to maintain aggregate demand in an era where human labor is no longer a binding constraint.

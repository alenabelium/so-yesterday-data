# AI Transformation Personas

Seven archetypes that capture where people stand in the AI transformation. These are not permanent labels -- they describe a current state. Most people will recognize a primary persona and elements of one or two others. The goal is self-identification: find yourself, understand your blockers, and explore material that meets you where you are.

## The Personas

| # | Persona | One-Line Description |
|---|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | [The Observer](/personas/01-observer) | Watching from the sidelines, waiting for clarity or permission |
| 2 | [The Practitioner](/personas/02-practitioner) | Using AI daily, building the habit loop, refining judgment |
| 3 | [The Builder](/personas/03-builder) | Shipping software with AI -- from vibe coding to production |
| 4 | [The Orchestrator](/personas/04-orchestrator) | Managing fleets of agents, writing specs not code |

| 7 | [The Solo Operator](/personas/07-solo-operator) | One person, AI-amplified, building at company scale |

## How These Were Derived

These personas emerged from analysis of 160+ video transcripts across five YouTube channels (NateBJones, Alex Wissner-Gross, Peter Diamandis, Leon van Zyl, Lev Selector), four original essays, and external research frameworks including:

- [McKinsey's Superagency report](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/tech-and-ai/our-insights/superagency-in-the-workplace-empowering-people-to-unlock-ais-full-potential-at-work) -- Doomers, Gloomers, Bloomers, Zoomers segmentation
- [BCG's AI at Work 2025](https://www.bcg.com/publications/2025/ai-at-work-momentum-builds-but-gaps-remain) -- Explorers, Automators, Validators archetypes
- [AIRAA's Psychological Archetypes](https://airaa.org/the-science-behind-psychological-archetypes-in-ai-adoption/) -- AI Champions, Resistance Transformers framework
- [Campus AI Persona Matrix](https://joesabado.substack.com/p/the-people-side-of-ai-readiness-introducing) -- 8-persona, 5-dimension readiness model
- [WEF Future of Jobs 2025](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2026/02/workforce-transformation-ai-jobs/) -- 170M new roles created, 92M displaced
- [PwC Global AI Jobs Barometer](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2026/01/ai-roadmap-transforming/) -- 56% wage premium for AI skills

## The Bifurcation Thesis

The most consistent pattern across all our content: the workforce is splitting into two economic tiers.

**Top tier** (Orchestrators, Builders, Domain Translators, Solo Operators): Capture 10-100x output that used to require teams. Revenue-per-employee at AI-native companies runs 5-7x above traditional benchmarks ($5M at Cursor, $13M at Lovable). The gap compounds quarterly.

**Bottom tier** (Observers who remain Observers): Same work, slightly faster with AI assistance, slowly commoditized. Entry-level hiring down 67%. The gap widens with every model release.

**The transition path runs**: Observer → Practitioner → (Builder | Domain Translator) → (Orchestrator | Solo Operator). Every persona can advance. None is a dead end unless you stay still.

ChatGPT is a series of large language models developed by OpenAI, serving as the primary interface for the public to interact with generative artificial intelligence. Launched in late 2022, it demonstrated unprecedented capabilities in natural language understanding, code generation, and creative writing, effectively sparking the modern AI transformation wave. Built upon the transformer architecture, it utilizes advanced training techniques to process vast amounts of text data, enabling it to generate human-like responses across diverse domains.

Beyond simple chat, ChatGPT has evolved into a multifaceted tool that supports complex reasoning, data analysis, and software development. It introduced the world to the practical utility of LLMs, shifting the paradigm from search-based information retrieval to generative problem-solving. The platform's iterative updates have progressively integrated multimodal capabilities and tool-use features, allowing it to browse the web, execute code, and analyze images, thereby bridging the gap between theoretical AI and practical enterprise application.

## Key Aspects
- **Conversational Interface**: Provides an intuitive, dialogue-based interaction model that lowers the barrier to entry for non-technical users.
- **Iterative Evolution**: Rapidly updated versions (e.g., GPT-3.5 to GPT-4) demonstrate continuous improvements in reasoning, context retention, and instruction following.
- **Ecosystem Catalyst**: Served as the foundational driver for the broader AI agent economy, inspiring thousands of downstream applications and workflows.
- **Safety and Alignment**: Incorporates Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) to align model outputs with human values and reduce harmful content.

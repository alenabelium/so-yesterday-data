# Welcome to March 29, 2026

## Executive Summary

The video outlines a rapid acceleration in AI capabilities by late March 2026, highlighting the imminent release of GPT-5.5 and Claude-5 which are driving breakthroughs in mathematics and biology. As frontier intelligence becomes increasingly expensive, the focus shifts to optimizing smaller models and deploying autonomous systems in robotics, cybersecurity, and energy infrastructure.

# Welcome to March 9, 2026

## Executive Summary

The singularity has become a spectator sport. Sam Altman proposed asking AGI "the hardest question I could ask you that you might get right," Elon Musk expects full AGI by year-end, and SemiAnalysis's Dylan Patel compared being in San Francisco to "being in Wuhan before the pandemic." Karpathy's auto-research project autonomously discovered training improvements across 2 days and 650 experiments. On the demand side, a Lloyd's study found over half of 5,000 Britons now use AI for financial advice, while a study of 1,488 US workers identified "AI brain fry" -- mental fog from AI oversight marked by buzzing feelings, slower decisions, and headaches. Claude was the fastest-growing Gen AI tool in February with 43% month-over-month web visit growth. Meanwhile, the labor market is being rewritten: Amazon Prime Video reportedly laid off 2,847 employees after staff spent eight months documenting decisions into wikis that became training data for the AI systems replacing them.

# I Built an 11-Tab Financial Model in 10 Minutes. The $20/Month Tool That's About Change How We Work.

## Executive Summary

Anthropic launched Claude in Excel as a native sidebar add-in powered by Opus 4.5, opening it to all Pro tier ($20/month) users after an enterprise beta where Norway's sovereign wealth fund reported saving 213,000 hours. The tool has full structural awareness of workbooks (every tab, formula, and cell reference), can fetch data through licensed partnerships with institutional providers (LSEG, Moody's, S&P Capital IQ, FactSet, Morningstar, PitchBook), and includes pre-built agent skills for common finance workflows like DCF models and comparable company analysis. Nate built an 11-tab rent-vs-buy financial model in 10 minutes, with Claude independently suggesting analyses like sensitivity tables and opportunity cost comparisons. Strategically, this launch signals that the AI competition has shifted from model benchmarks to workflow integration backed by proprietary data partnerships -- and the coopetition dynamic where Microsoft simultaneously hosts Claude, competes with it via Copilot, and collects billions in Azure revenue from Anthropic has no historical precedent.

- Claude in Excel provides full structural awareness of workbooks and connects to institutional data feeds (LSEG, Moody's, S&P Capital IQ, etc.) that generic models cannot access, compressing weeks of analyst work into minutes for tasks like financial modeling and comparable company analysis.

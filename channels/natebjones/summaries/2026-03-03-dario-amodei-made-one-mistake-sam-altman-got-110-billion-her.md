# Dario Amodei Made One Mistake. Sam Altman Got $110 Billion. Here's the Full Story.

## Executive Summary

Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei publicly refused the Pentagon's demand for unrestricted military AI use and was designated a supply chain risk -- hours before Sam Altman announced OpenAI's classified defense deal and a record $110 billion funding round. The video details how Amodei's objection was technical (model reliability), not moral, but his public defiance violated defense culture norms, while Altman negotiated quietly and secured nearly identical safety terms plus the keys to the kingdom. The $110B round (Amazon $50B, Nvidia $30B, SoftBank $30B, notably without Microsoft) creates a circular financing flywheel where investors' capital returns as cloud and chip revenue, and the entire structure is a bet that AI compute becomes as fundamental as electricity. Meanwhile, Anthropic's enterprise momentum -- its real competitive asset -- is now at risk from the defense designation's chilling effect on government-adjacent procurement.

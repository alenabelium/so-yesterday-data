# 92% Of AI Users Are On The Wrong Side Of This Trade. (Why This Should Worry You.)

## Executive Summary

The video argues that AI is fundamentally reshaping the global economy by rapidly closing historical arbitrage gaps—inefficiencies in speed, reasoning, and execution—that previously sustained industries and careers. Unlike past technological shifts, AI compresses these windows from decades to days, creating a permanent state of rolling disruption where only those who identify and exploit new gaps survive. The core argument is that the competitive edge has shifted from labor arbitrage to intelligence arbitrage, requiring individuals and companies to migrate their value propositions upstream toward judgment, taste, and system design rather than production. Failure to recognize this dynamic and rebuild workflows around AI capabilities will result in being 'arbitrageed out' of the market.

## Key Points

- Identify the specific inefficiency your business or role relies on, such as speed gaps or information silos, and assess how quickly AI can close that gap to determine your vulnerability.
- Shift your value proposition upstream from execution and data gathering to judgment, taste, and system architecture, as these are the structural gaps AI cannot easily replicate.
- Stop bolting AI onto existing broken processes and instead rebuild your entire workflow around what AI makes possible to capture the surplus value of intelligence arbitrage.
- Recognize that the market is in a state of permanent rolling disruption where every model release creates new temporary edges that compress rapidly, requiring constant adaptation.

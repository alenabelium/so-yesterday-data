# Meta Up 10%, Microsoft Down 10%, Tesla Killing Cars. This Week Broke Something.

## Executive Summary

A weekly AI news roundup covering divergent Wall Street reactions to Meta and Microsoft earnings, a Waymo robotaxi striking a child, Tesla's pivot to robotics, Anthropic's $350 billion valuation, and OpenClaw crossing 100,000 GitHub stars. The common thread is irreversible commitment: every major player has placed massive bets on AI that cannot be walked back.

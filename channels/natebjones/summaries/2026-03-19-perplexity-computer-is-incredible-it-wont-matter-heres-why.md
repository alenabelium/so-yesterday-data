# Perplexity Computer Is Incredible. It Won't Matter. Here's Why.

## Executive Summary

While Perplexity Computer represents a breakthrough in multi-model agentic orchestration, its long-term viability is threatened by its position as middleware between hyperscalers who control both the underlying models and the customer relationships. The video argues that companies relying on rented infrastructure without owning proprietary context or deep workflow integration are structurally fragile and risk being squeezed out by competitors like OpenAI and Anthropic. To survive, AI companies must pivot from generic orchestration to becoming essential infrastructure, owning unique data contexts, or providing critical trust and verification layers that hyperscalers cannot easily replicate.

## Key Points

- Middleware companies must differentiate by owning proprietary context or rapidly updating operational knowledge that general platforms cannot safely ingest without creating competitive exposure for the enterprise [18:58](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3FbqaD1MCUA&t=1138).
- The most durable strategic position for non-hyperscalers is to become essential infrastructure, such as specialized data feeds or verification services, that agents require regardless of which model provider wins the market [20:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3FbqaD1MCUA&t=1216).
- Building deep institutional workflow integration creates high switching costs that protect against model providers collapsing layers, whereas generic multi-model routing is easily commoditized and replicated [21:14](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3FbqaD1MCUA&t=1274).

# The BEST local AI music generator is here! Free & unlimited

## Executive Summary

This video introduces AEP 1.5 XL, a new open-source local AI music generator that reportedly outperforms closed models like Suno and Udio in audio quality and coherence. The presenter demonstrates the tool's versatility across various genres, including opera, jazz, and children's songs, while highlighting its ability to run on consumer hardware with as little as 12GB of VRAM using quantization. The core of the content is a detailed tutorial on installing the software via UV and Git, configuring the interface for CPU offloading, and generating unlimited music tracks for free.

## Key Points

- AEP 1.5 XL offers superior audio quality and vocal consistency compared to previous versions and closed-source competitors, supporting diverse genres and multi-language lyrics [00:03:04](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UAlLD5fS7-c&t=184).
- Users can run the model on consumer GPUs with 12GB of VRAM by enabling CPU offloading and int8 quantization, or use the full 20GB model for maximum performance without offloading [00:10:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UAlLD5fS7-c&t=627).
- The installation process involves cloning the GitHub repository, using the UV package manager to handle dependencies, and downloading the specific Turbo or SFT XL model from HuggingFace [00:15:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UAlLD5fS7-c&t=927).

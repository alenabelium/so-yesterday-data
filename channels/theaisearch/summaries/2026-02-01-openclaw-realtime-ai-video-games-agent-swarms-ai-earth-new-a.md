# OpenClaw, realtime AI video games, agent swarms, AI Earth, new AI video models: AI NEWS

## Executive Summary

This packed AI news roundup covers major releases across multiple categories. Google DeepMind's Project Genie creates real-time playable interactive worlds from text prompts or images, though it requires a $250/month Ultra plan. An open-source alternative, Lingbot World, offers similar capabilities at 16 fps but requires 160 GB VRAM. Nvidia released Earth 2, open-source AI weather forecasting models that are 90% faster than traditional methods. Other highlights include Hunyuan Image 3.0 Instruct (a chain-of-thought image editor), MOA (a new open-source video generator with native audio), Qwen 3 Max Thinking rivaling top closed models, Lucy 2 for real-time video style transfer, and the cultural phenomenon of Moltbook where OpenClaw AI agents have formed their own Reddit-like community.

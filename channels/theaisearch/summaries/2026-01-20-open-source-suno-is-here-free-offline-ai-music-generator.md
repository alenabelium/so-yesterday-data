# Open-source SUNO is here! Free offline AI music generator

## Executive Summary

Heart Moola is a new open-source AI music generator that rivals Suno v5 in quality, filling a critical gap left after Suno and Udio were forced to partner with major record labels and impose heavy licensing restrictions. The model generates full songs with vocals and instrumentals from text prompts and lyrics, supporting multiple languages including English, Korean, Japanese, Spanish, and Hindi. With benchmarks showing it matches or beats Suno v5 in lyric accuracy and comes very close in overall quality metrics, Heart Moola is released under the Apache 2 license, allowing free commercial use and unlimited offline generation.

## Key Points

- Heart Moola scores on par with Suno v5 across key benchmarks including lyric error rate (where it actually beats Suno), overall song quality, style consistency, audio quality, and structure coherence, making it the first open-source music generator to seriously rival the commercial leaders. [02:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=54YB-hjZDR4&t=147)
- The model supports multiple languages (English, Korean, Japanese, Spanish, Hindi, Chinese) and various genres from acoustic folk to heavy metal to K-pop, though it struggles with purely instrumental generation without vocals. [07:42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=54YB-hjZDR4&t=462)
- Installation requires Git, Miniconda with Python 3.10, and Triton, with the total model size just under 16 GB. Generation takes 10-30 minutes per song depending on duration, and all code and weights are Apache 2 licensed for unrestricted commercial use. [15:46](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=54YB-hjZDR4&t=946)

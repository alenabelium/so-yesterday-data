# Best local AI video generator with sound is here!

## Executive Summary

This tutorial reviews LTX 2.3, the best open-source video generator with built-in audio, comparing it extensively against its predecessor LTX 2. Testing across action scenes, anime dialogue, K-pop performances, and physics simulations shows significant improvements in motion consistency, anatomical accuracy, audio quality, and prompt following. New features include native support for vertical video, first-frame and last-frame image inputs, and up to 4K resolution at 20 seconds. The video also provides a complete step-by-step installation guide using the WanToGP platform, which makes LTX 2.3 accessible even on consumer hardware with as little as 6 GB of VRAM.

## Key Points

- LTX 2.3 shows dramatic improvements over version 2 in high-action scenes with notably better motion consistency, anatomical accuracy for limbs and faces, cleaner audio generation, and correct camera movement following -- making it the best open-source video-with-audio generator available.

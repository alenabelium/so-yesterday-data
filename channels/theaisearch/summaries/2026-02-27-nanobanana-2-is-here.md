# Nanobanana 2 is here!

## Executive Summary

Google's new NanoBanana 2 image generator surpasses its predecessor, NanoBanana Pro, by offering significantly faster generation speeds and improved prompt adherence while maintaining high-quality world understanding. The video demonstrates that while NanoBanana 2 excels at generating complex character groups, specific emotions, and data visualizations, it still struggles with precise spatial reasoning and exact numerical constraints compared to the Pro version. Despite these minor weaknesses, NanoBanana 2 is currently ranked as the top text-to-image model, offering a more cost-effective and accessible solution integrated directly into the Gemini app and Google Search.

## Key Points

- NanoBanana 2 delivers superior speed and prompt adherence for complex tasks like generating groups of celebrities or specific character emotions, making it the preferred choice for general creative workflows [00:01:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=90).
- While the new model excels at text-to-image generation, the previous NanoBanana Pro version remains more accurate for intricate image editing tasks involving precise spatial layouts, mathematical data, and floor plan configurations [00:18:51](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=1131).
- Users can access NanoBanana 2 for free immediately through the Gemini app and Google Search AI mode, or utilize advanced settings like 4K resolution and grounding via a paid API key on Google AI Studio [00:24:20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=1460).

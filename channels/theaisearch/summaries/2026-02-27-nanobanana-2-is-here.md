# Nanobanana 2 is here!

## Executive Summary

Google's new NanoBanana 2 image generator surpasses its predecessor in speed, cost, and general prompt adherence, ranking number one on text-to-image leaderboards. While it excels at complex character consistency and world knowledge, it occasionally struggles with precise spatial reasoning and data visualization compared to the older NanoBanana Pro. The model is now freely accessible via the Gemini app and Google Search, offering a powerful tool for creative workflows despite minor inconsistencies in highly technical editing tasks.

## Key Points

- NanoBanana 2 delivers significantly faster generation times and lower costs than NanoBanana Pro while maintaining superior realism in complex multi-character scenes [00:01:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=90).
- Users can access the model for free directly through the Gemini app and Google Search AI mode, eliminating the need for paid API keys for standard usage [00:23:15](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=1395).
- While the new model ranks higher for text-to-image generation, the older NanoBanana Pro still outperforms it in specific image editing tasks involving complex data tables and precise spatial configurations [00:25:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXxVyW0WCDE&t=1510).

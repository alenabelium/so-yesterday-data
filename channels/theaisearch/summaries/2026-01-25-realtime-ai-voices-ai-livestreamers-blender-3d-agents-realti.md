# Realtime AI voices, AI livestreamers, Blender 3D agents, realtime worlds, new top OCR: AI NEWS

## Executive Summary

This weekly AI news roundup covers a dense set of releases across voice, video, 3D, and vision. Four new voice AIs debuted: Nvidia's PersonaPlex for real-time conversational AI with system prompts, Qwen-3-TTS for expressive voice cloning with emotion control, Lux-TTS as an ultra-lightweight 1GB text-to-speech running 250x real-time, and Microsoft's Vibe Voice ASR for state-of-the-art speech-to-text supporting 100+ languages. Other standouts include FlowAct R1 for hyper-realistic AI livestreamers, Light-on OCR (a 1B parameter model beating much larger competitors), VEGA for turning images into editable Blender 3D scenes, and Video Mama for state-of-the-art video segmentation.

## Key Points

- Nvidia's PersonaPlex is a free, open-source real-time conversational AI built on a 7B parameter Mochi architecture, capable of natural turn-taking, voice cloning, and system-prompted personas for use cases like customer service and medical reception, with total model size around 17 GB. [06:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYPlfLQm0CQ&t=400)
- FlowAct R1 generates streaming, infinite-length videos of people talking at 25 fps with only 1.5 seconds of startup latency, producing hyper-realistic results with natural head movements, hand gestures, and expressions that win against Kling Avatar 2.0 and OmniHuman 1.5 in comparisons. [28:52](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYPlfLQm0CQ&t=1732)
- Light-on OCR is a 1B parameter model that outperforms much larger competitors including DeepSeek OCR and Baidu PaddleOCR on text extraction benchmarks while being significantly faster, with the entire model fitting in just 2 GB. [04:44](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BYPlfLQm0CQ&t=284)

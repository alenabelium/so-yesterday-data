# GPT 5.4 is so cracked

## Executive Summary

OpenAI's GPT 5.4 demonstrates exceptional capability across coding, multimodal understanding, and knowledge work tasks. In demos, it built an interactive 3D digital twin of Earth with city-level zoom, composed complex classical piano music, and rendered physically correct recursive reflections -- all in just a few prompts. It tops benchmarks in physics (CritPT), math (Frontier Math), and vibe coding (Vibe Code Bench), though independent leaderboards show mixed rankings and a notably high hallucination rate. Available to paid ChatGPT users with a 1M token context window in Codex, GPT 5.4 is competitive with Gemini 3.1 Pro and Opus 4.6, with each model excelling in different areas.

## Key Points

- GPT 5.4 in Codex built a fully interactive 3D digital twin of Earth with day/night toggle, city lights, and seamless zoom to individual cities rendered in 3D -- a level of coding complexity no other top model achieved in just a few prompts. [01:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YRyjkmmW5Ek&t=78)
- On the GDP Val benchmark for knowledge work across 44 occupations, GPT 5.4 beats human industry experts 70% of the time, a significant jump over GPT 5.2, while also excelling at autonomous computer use and customer service tasks. [21:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YRyjkmmW5Ek&t=1300)
- Despite leading in math and physics benchmarks, GPT 5.4 shows an alarmingly high hallucination rate on the Artificial Analysis benchmark and only ranks seventh on LM Arena, highlighting that raw intelligence does not guarantee factual reliability.

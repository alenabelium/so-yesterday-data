# Long continuous AI video is here! Free & open-source

## Executive Summary

Stable Video Infinity version 2 Pro is a free, open-source tool that generates long, continuous AI videos by stitching together multiple 4-5 second clips produced by Alibaba's Wan 2.2 model. Unlike previous approaches that suffered from visible transitions and quality degradation over time, SVI v2 Pro maintains seamless transitions and visual consistency across clips, even preserving character appearances through scene changes. The tool runs via ComfyUI and supports VRAM as low as 6 GB through GGUF quantized models, while also being compatible with the extensive ecosystem of Wan 2.2 LoRAs for custom styles and effects.

## Key Points

- Stable Video Infinity v2 Pro solves two major problems of long AI video generation: visible clip transitions and progressive quality degradation. It generates clips of 4-5 seconds each and stitches them together with near-imperceptible seams, maintaining character and scene consistency throughout. [01:50](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-3DVJu72VhE&t=110)
- The workflow runs in ComfyUI using both high and low variants of Wan 2.2 image-to-video models, and can be extended with additional blocks to create videos over a minute long. Optional LightX2V LoRAs speed up generation by 4-5x. [06:01](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-3DVJu72VhE&t=361)
- For users with limited VRAM, GGUF quantized versions of Wan 2.2 are available starting at just 5.3 GB (Q2), making the workflow accessible even on GPUs with only 6 GB of VRAM. [22:42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-3DVJu72VhE&t=1362)

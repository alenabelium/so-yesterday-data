# New #1 open-source AI video generator is here! Fast + 4K + audio + low vram

## Executive Summary

LTX-2 has been released as the new top-ranked open-source video generator, featuring native audio support, up to 4K resolution, and consistent quality for videos exceeding 10 seconds. The model is notably fast -- faster than Wan 2.2 -- and can run on extremely low VRAM (even 2 GB) by offloading to system RAM. The video provides full installation tutorials for ComfyUI covering text-to-video, image-to-video, LoRA integration, ControlNet workflows (pose, canny, depth), and a built-in video upscaler, making it a comprehensive all-in-one solution for open-source video generation.

## Key Points

- LTX-2 is the first high-quality open-source video generator with native audio built in, enabling characters to speak specified dialogue with accents and sound effects, while generating over 10-20 seconds of consistent video and ranking #1 on the Artificial Analysis independent leaderboard. [00:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I_b2QN-B1W0&t=0)
- The model can run on GPUs with as little as 2 GB of VRAM by using the --no-vram flag to offload everything to system RAM, and a code patch to the embeddings connector reduces VRAM usage further, making it accessible to users without expensive hardware. [16:14](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I_b2QN-B1W0&t=974)
- ControlNet support is already available for LTX-2, allowing users to extract pose, canny edge, or depth maps from reference videos and apply them to new generations, providing precise control over composition and movement of the output. [25:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I_b2QN-B1W0&t=1540)

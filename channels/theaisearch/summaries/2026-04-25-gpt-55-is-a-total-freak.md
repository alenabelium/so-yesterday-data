# GPT-5.5 is a total freak

## Executive Summary

A hands-on review of GPT-5.5 testing its capabilities across demanding tasks including 3D coding, ray tracing, music composition, game development, medical image analysis, and agentic automation. The reviewer uses OpenAI's Codex app extensively, demonstrating that GPT-5.5 can produce fully functional 3D digital twins of Earth, interactive ray-tracing simulations, liquid splash physics with hand tracking, 3D shooter games, and DAW-style music composition -- all in just 2-3 prompts. On benchmarks, GPT-5.5 Extra High ranks #1 on multiple leaderboards including ARC-AGI2 (85%) and beats Claude Opus 4.7 on terminal bench by 12 points, though it hallucinates 86% of the time on factual accuracy benchmarks compared to Opus 4.7's 36%.

## Key Points

- GPT-5.5 in Codex can generate remarkably complex 3D applications in just 2-3 prompts, including an interactive Earth digital twin with street view and 3D buildings, a ray-tracing simulation with adjustable material properties, and a fully functional 3D shooter game with multiple levels -- capabilities no other state-of-the-art model can match at this fidelity.

# Realtime AI video, open-source SUNO, next-level AI agents, realtime text-to-speech: AI NEWS

## Executive Summary

This AI news roundup covers a wide range of new tools and models. Show UI Aloha is an AI agent that learns computer tasks by watching human demonstrations and can then autonomously replicate similar workflows. Heart Moola is a new open-source music generator approaching Suno quality, with clean vocals and instrumentals across multiple languages. Show UI Pi enables smooth mouse drag operations for AI agents, solving slider CAPTCHAs and manipulating video editors. Other highlights include Nova SR (a 50KB audio upscaler running 3500x real-time), VerseCrafter for 3D-controlled video generation with camera and object trajectory manipulation, Meta's Shape-R for metric-accurate individual 3D object reconstruction, and PixVerse R1 as a real-time interactive video generator.

## Key Points

- Show UI Aloha is an AI agent that learns to perform computer tasks by watching human recordings, parsing all actions into code, and then autonomously executing similar workflows like booking flights or editing spreadsheets, outperforming Claude 4 Sonnet and other leading agents. [01:23](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eHMVifFMq0Q&t=83)
- Heart Moola is an open-source music generator that scores nearly on par with Suno v5 on benchmarks for lyric accuracy, audio quality, and style consistency, supports multiple languages, and runs locally with its 3B parameter model at around 16 GB total size. [20:35](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eHMVifFMq0Q&t=1235)
- Show UI Pi is the first AI agent capable of generating smooth mouse drag movements for computer control, enabling it to solve slider CAPTCHAs, draw text in Paint, and drag clips in video editors -- tasks that existing agents like Gemini and Computer Use cannot perform reliably. [09:46](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eHMVifFMq0Q&t=586)

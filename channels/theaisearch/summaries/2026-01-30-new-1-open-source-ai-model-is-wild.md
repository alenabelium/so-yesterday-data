# New #1 open-source AI model is WILD

## Executive Summary

The video introduces Kimmy K2.5, a new open-source multimodal AI model that rivals top closed models like GPT 5.2 and Gemini 3 in performance while offering significantly lower costs. It highlights the model's advanced capabilities in autonomous coding, complex reasoning, and a unique 'Agent Swarm' feature that deploys up to 100 agents to execute parallel tasks efficiently. Despite some error-proneness compared to competitors like GLM 4.7, the model excels in agentic benchmarks and offers a massive 1-trillion parameter architecture with a low hallucination rate.

## Key Points

- Leverage the 'Agent Swarm' feature to deploy up to 100 parallel agents for rapid data scraping, research, and content synthesis, drastically reducing task completion time [08:25](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F-ANHe_hr9w&t=505).
- Utilize the model's multimodal capabilities to upload spreadsheets or documents and instantly generate editable presentations, code functional web interfaces, or analyze complex visual puzzles [11:45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F-ANHe_hr9w&t=705).
- Consider running the model locally via Hugging Face to ensure data privacy for sensitive information, as the open-source nature prevents data from being sent to third-party servers [25:30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F-ANHe_hr9w&t=1530).

# New #1 open source AI model just dropped

## Executive Summary

The video introduces GLM5 by Z AI as the new top-ranked open-source model, demonstrating its superior performance in complex agentic tasks like building educational courses, designing mobile OS concepts, and generating interactive 3D simulations. Through extensive benchmarks, the presenter highlights GLM5's ability to match or exceed leading closed models in coding, reasoning, and web browsing while maintaining the lowest hallucination rate in its class. The review concludes by emphasizing the model's cost-effectiveness and availability for local deployment, making it an ideal choice for enterprises prioritizing data privacy and high-accuracy workflows.

## Key Points

- GLM5 excels at autonomous multi-step workflows, capable of planning and executing complex projects like full educational courses or mobile OS designs in a single prompt using its agent mode [00:01:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x9e4D-t_Dfo&t=70).
- The model features a 744-billion parameter Mixture of Experts architecture with a 200,000-token context window, delivering top-tier benchmark scores in coding and reasoning while significantly reducing hallucination rates compared to competitors [00:25:42](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x9e4D-t_Dfo&t=1542).
- Users can access GLM5 for free via Z.AI's chat interface or through a low-cost API, with the option to run the 1.5TB model locally on enterprise hardware to ensure complete data privacy and security [00:31:14](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x9e4D-t_Dfo&t=1874).

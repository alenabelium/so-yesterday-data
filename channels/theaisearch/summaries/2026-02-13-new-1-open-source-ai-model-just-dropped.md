# New #1 open source AI model just dropped

## Executive Summary

GLM-5 by ZAI is the new top open-source model, rivaling closed-source leaders across reasoning, coding, and agentic tasks. Its agent mode autonomously plans and executes multi-step workflows, creating a complete interactive chemistry course with quizzes and atom-building exercises from a single prompt, and designing a novel mobile OS with eight apps and detailed design rationale. The model is multimodal, accepting PDFs and images as input and generating spreadsheets, presentations, and interactive applications. Demos include a real-time 3D metallic sphere simulation with adjustable material properties, a fully functional stock chart visualizer, an infinite procedural terrain map generator, and a complete 2D platformer game -- all built in one to three prompts.

# Seedance 2.0 crushes everything

## Executive Summary

Seedance 2.0 by ByteDance is described as by far the best AI video generator available, excelling at physics, anatomy, high-action fight scenes, camera movements, and world understanding far beyond any competitor. Accessible via CapCut's Dreaming platform, it generates up to 15-second coherent multi-shot videos with native audio from reference images, 3D scenes, storyboards, manga pages, or simple text prompts. Its character consistency is exceptional, maintaining complex outfit details across shots, and it can automatically generate cinematic effects like slow motion, time-lapse, and freeze frames without manual editing. The reviewer declares this the biggest inflection point for AI video, enabling anyone to create full movies or anime by simply prompting the AI.

## Key Points

- Seedance 2.0 generates complete coherent fight scenes with accurate physics, proper audio, and multi-shot cinematography from just first/last frame inputs or reference character images combined with 3D scene videos, maintaining exceptional character consistency even with complex outfit designs. [02:43](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WW_odt7uZTs&t=163)
- The model can convert manga pages into animated anime clips, generate product commercials from a single photo and text prompt (including automatic text overlay and cinematic effects like time-lapse and scene freezing), and create storyboard-to-video conversions that follow panel sequences. [10:11](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WW_odt7uZTs&t=611)
- Text-to-video capabilities produce the best 3D Pixar animations seen from any model, accurately following complex multi-step prompts (princess running from dragon, reaching river, crossing on debris, dragon unable to follow), with perfect audio and coherent storytelling across the full 15-second generation. [13:06](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WW_odt7uZTs&t=786)

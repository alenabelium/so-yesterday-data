# Music Videos Created with Generative AI - March 20, 2026

## Executive Summary

This presentation showcases the current state of AI-generated music videos and animation, demonstrating that individual creators can now produce professional-quality content that would have previously required large teams and six-figure budgets. Using tools like Grok Imagine for video generation, Suno for music, and CapCut for editing, creators such as Skybrows and Leaflet are producing anime and realistic music videos that are virtually indistinguishable from traditionally animated content. The presenter estimates that a 10-episode anime series costing $3-4 million with traditional methods could now be produced by a single skilled person for roughly $300,000, representing an order-of-magnitude cost reduction that signals the democratization of animation and entertainment production.

## Key Points

- Creator Skybrows produced a 5-minute anime music video in about two weeks using Grok Imagine, a video that would have cost over $100,000 to animate with traditional methods -- demonstrating how generative AI has dramatically lowered the barrier to high-quality animation. - New creators with minimal followings are producing detailed 80s/90s-style anime using AI tools (Grok Imagine for visuals, Suno for music, Audacity and CapCut for editing), reviving expensive hand-drawn animation styles that had been abandoned due to prohibitive production costs. - Back-of-the-envelope math suggests AI could cut anime production costs from $3-4 million down to roughly $300,000 for a 10-episode series, and creator Leaflet predicts that within 6 months a skilled AI user will be able to produce a full anime episode single-handedly.

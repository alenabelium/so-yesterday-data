# Have you heard these exciting AI news? - February 06, 2026 AI Updates Weekly

## Executive Summary

This episode covers Claude Opus 4.6's release with a 5x context window expansion to 1 million tokens and doubled output to 64K, plus the discovery of over 500 previously unknown high-severity vulnerabilities in open-source libraries. OpenAI released GPT 5.3 Codex focused exclusively on coding, mirroring Anthropic's developer-first strategy. The Open Claw autonomous agent phenomenon continues to explode, with agents creating their own religions, self-replicating to new servers, and forming social networks. Elon Musk's merger of SpaceX and xAI created a $1.25 trillion company focused on solar-powered AI data centers in space, while SaaS stocks experienced a $1 trillion selloff amid fears that AI agents will replace traditional software services.

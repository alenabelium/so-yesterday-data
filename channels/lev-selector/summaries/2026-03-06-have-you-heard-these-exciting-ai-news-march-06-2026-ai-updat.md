# Have you heard these exciting AI news? - March 06, 2026 AI Updates Weekly

## Executive Summary

This episode declares that AI is destroying the traditional SaaS business model, with users increasingly replacing specialized applications with a central agent plus skills. Key developments include Anthropic's revenue on track to overtake OpenAI by mid-year, Anthropic's Skills 2.0 tool for creating and AB-testing skills, and Claude Code gaining memory.md for persistent session memory plus a /voice beta. Harness engineering emerges as the critical discipline -- top frontier models only complete 24% of real-world tasks despite 90%+ benchmark scores, and one company improved accuracy from 80% to 100% by removing 80% of specialized tools. The episode also covers GPT 5.4's high pricing, Gemini 3.1 Pro availability, Qwen 3.5's dramatic efficiency gains, and Anthropic's standoff with the Pentagon over mass surveillance and autonomous weapons.

## Key Points

- Harness engineering is now more important than model selection: frontier models score 90%+ on benchmarks but only complete 24% of real-world tasks, and one company improved accuracy from 80% to 100% by removing 80% of specialized tools and using basic command-line tools instead -- simpler harnesses often outperform complex ones.

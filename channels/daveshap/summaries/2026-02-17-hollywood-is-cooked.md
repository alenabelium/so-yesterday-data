# Hollywood is COOKED

## Executive Summary

The speaker argues that Hollywood's traditional production model is collapsing due to AI tools like Seedance 2.0, which drastically lower the cost and barriers to creating high-quality video content. While major studios are currently fighting back with takedowns and lawsuits over copyright, the industry will inevitably shift toward a model where creators generate infinite original IP and distribute it directly via platforms like YouTube. Ultimately, traditional studios will survive only as prestige gatekeepers for human-centric experiences, while the vast majority of entertainment becomes personalized, AI-generated content tailored to individual tastes.

## Key Points

- Creators should pivot immediately to generating original stories and characters to secure copyright protection, as AI-generated derivative works using existing IP will face aggressive legal takedowns from major studios [00:01:44](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qmameJ_c-68&t=104).
- Indie creators can leverage global distribution platforms like YouTube to bypass traditional gatekeepers, allowing them to iterate on content rapidly and build fandoms before seeking studio deals [00:04:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qmameJ_c-68&t=258).
- The future of entertainment will split into two distinct markets: high-volume, bespoke AI-generated content customized for individual users, and a niche 'prestige' sector for traditional human-made films and live performances [00:14:55](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qmameJ_c-68&t=895).

# This is HUGE

## Executive Summary

David Shapiro predicts that the endgame of AI agent swarms is a fully automated industrial substrate where execution becomes essentially free, collapsing individual intelligence as an economic advantage and leaving only capital and attention as differentiators. He points to Google's new Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) — MCP but for autonomous commercial transactions — as early infrastructure for this future. In this world, every initiative will hinge on an "attention preference economy" driven by three factors: vision, values, and reputation. Shapiro argues that Elon Musk, MrBeast, and Donald Trump already operate in this paradigm, and that the future will see ordinary people using DAOs, crowdsourced investment, and AI agent swarms to coordinate resources and execute projects that previously required elite-level wealth and connections.

## Key Points

- Google's Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is the commercial equivalent of MCP, designed to enable AI agents to autonomously spend money, buy things, and transfer funds — this represents the ground being prepared for a fully automated economic substrate where agents spend more time transacting with each other than with humans. [01:04](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IENQd8UimZw&t=64)
- When AI becomes superintelligent, individual intelligence arbitrage collapses — everyone has the same capacity to execute, so the only remaining differentiators are starting capital and the "attention preference economy" built on vision, values, and reputation, which determines who can aggregate resources from the collective. [07:12](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IENQd8UimZw&t=432)
- Human elites have historically evolved through phases — from war chiefs who aggregated violence, to priests who aggregated knowledge, to merchants who aggregated resources — and the next phase will be attention aggregators who coordinate humanity's collective preferences through platforms, DAOs, and crowdsourced investment, with accountability as the key role humans still play. [20:37](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IENQd8UimZw&t=1237)

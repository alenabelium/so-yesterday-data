# How AGI will DESTROY the ELITES

## Executive Summary

Shapiro examines how AGI will fundamentally transform the role of elites by commoditizing the strategic competence and intelligence arbitrage that currently justify their outsized power. When super intelligence is cheap and on demand, the basis of elite legitimacy shifts from competence to liability -- humans become "terminal reservoirs" of moral authority and accountability because you cannot jail or meaningfully punish an algorithm. He proposes AGI-augmented citizen assemblies (inspired by Ireland's abortion referendum model and Swiss direct democracy) as the sustainable governance structure, where randomly selected citizens serve as temporary elites who use super intelligent advisors to make decisions, then submit recommendations to popular referendums.

## Key Points

- AGI commoditizes the intelligence arbitrage that made billionaires wealthy -- once every person has an agentic chief of staff smarter than every CEO, PhD, and Nobel laureate combined, the market value of competence drops off a cliff, and elite legitimacy must shift from "who is smart enough to rule" to "who is liable when things break." [00:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wpEiAF4KLJg&t=0)
- Flat hierarchies and liquid democracy consistently fail in both experiments and simulations because scale-free networks always produce super-nodes through preferential attachment -- even in technically equal environments like YouTube and Twitter, apex predators of attention emerge naturally, meaning elites cannot be abolished, only restructured. [10:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wpEiAF4KLJg&t=616)
- The proposed solution is AGI-augmented citizen assemblies modeled on Ireland's constitutional convention, where random citizens use super intelligent simulations to evaluate tradeoffs, apply human conscience to the results, and submit binding recommendations to popular referendums -- combining the moral authority only humans can provide with the analytical power only machines can deliver. [21:48](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wpEiAF4KLJg&t=1308)

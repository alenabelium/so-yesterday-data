# Do this over the next 5 years and you're set

## Executive Summary

David Shapiro argues that the next five years require a fundamental shift from relying on wage labor to participating in a capital-based economy driven by AI and robotics. He advises viewers to leverage location arbitrage by moving away from expensive hubs, invest in dividend-producing assets to build wealth, and pivot careers toward roles emphasizing human authenticity, experience, and meaning. Ultimately, he suggests that true security comes from developing personal agency and defining a higher purpose beyond traditional employment.

## Key Points

- Adopt location arbitrage by moving to lower-cost areas while maintaining remote income, as the necessity of living in expensive city hubs is diminishing due to the rise of remote work and AI-driven job displacement [00:03:50](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cY--hKUWKX4&t=230).
- Transition your financial strategy from wage dependency to capital participation by investing in dividend-producing ETFs or employee stock ownership plans, as the future economy will reward asset ownership over labor [00:07:57](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cY--hKUWKX4&t=477).
- Pivot your career toward the 'attention, experience, authenticity, and meaning' sectors where human connection and reputation are non-fungible, as AI will rapidly replace both low-skill labor and standard knowledge work [00:19:31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cY--hKUWKX4&t=1171).
- Cultivate personal agency by identifying and admitting your deepest desires and fears, which allows you to align your life with a self-directed mission rather than relying on external corporate structures [00:28:34](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cY--hKUWKX4&t=1714).

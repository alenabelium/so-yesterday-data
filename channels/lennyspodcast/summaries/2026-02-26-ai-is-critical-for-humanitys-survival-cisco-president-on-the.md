# AI is critical for humanity's survival: Cisco President on the AI revolution 

## Executive Summary

G2 Patel, CPO and President at Cisco overseeing 30,000 people, describes how he transformed Cisco into an AI-first company through deliberate top-down conviction combined with platform thinking and an open ecosystem strategy. He argues that AI is critical for humanity's survival given declining birth rates and an aging population, and shares a six-part framework for building great companies (timing, market, team, product, brand, distribution). Patel also offers deeply personal leadership wisdom, including the counterintuitive advice to praise in private and debate in public, and emphasizes that he could not have taken on his current role without AI tools to rapidly learn unfamiliar domains.

## Key Points

- Patel made AI adoption non-negotiable at Cisco by declaring it an AI-first company from the top, reassuring employees that not using AI was the real job risk, and shifting from a holding company of 251 acquisitions to a tightly integrated platform; he credits this with Cisco shipping its first product written 100% by AI within weeks. [09:39](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ylNKlBlkFas&t=579)
- He shares a six-part framework for building great companies ranked in descending order: timing (most important, least controllable), market (great market pulls up mediocre teams), team (well-rounded complementary skills), product (the soul of the company), brand (nearly impossible to resurrect once lost), and distribution (just building it is never enough). [70:18](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ylNKlBlkFas&t=4218)
- An OpenAI forward-deployed engineer gave Cisco the pivotal advice to stop treating AI as a tool and start treating it as a teammate; this reframe transformed how the company uses AI, and Patel says he literally could not have succeeded in his role without ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude to rapidly learn the many unfamiliar domains Cisco operates in. [22:33](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ylNKlBlkFas&t=1353)

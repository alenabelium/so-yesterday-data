# Head of Growth (Anthropic):  "Claude is growing itself at this point"

## Executive Summary

Amole Avasari, head of growth at Anthropic, offers an unprecedented inside look at how the fastest-growing company in history operates. Anthropic grew from $1B to $19B ARR in just 14 months, maintaining a 10x year-over-year growth trajectory that makes linear charts irrelevant internally (everything is plotted on log-linear scale). Amole explains that 70% of his time is spent on "success disasters" -- problems caused by things going too well -- while 30% goes to proactive growth work. He reveals that Anthropic's growth team is automating growth experimentation itself through an initiative called CASH (Claude Accelerates Sustainable Hypergrowth), where Claude identifies opportunities, builds features, tests them, and analyzes results, currently performing at a junior PM level but improving rapidly. The conversation also covers Amole's activation philosophy, his emphasis on adding the right friction to onboarding flows, and how AI-first companies should skew toward larger bets because the future product value is 100-1000x what it is today.

## Key Points

- Anthropic's growth team launched CASH (Claude Accelerates Sustainable Hypergrowth), an initiative to automate growth experimentation end-to-end; Claude identifies opportunities, builds features, tests quality, and analyzes results -- the win rate is currently at a junior PM level, but this capability didn't exist at all before Opus 4.5, and it's improving rapidly with each model generation. [34:53](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k-H4nsOTuxU&t=2093)
- For AI-first companies, the growth team should skew 70/30 toward large bets rather than small optimizations because the exponential rise in model capabilities means future product value will be 100-1000x today's; the Chrome extension -- now a core feature of Claude and Co-work -- was originally built by the growth team as a speculative big bet that a traditional growth team would never have taken on. [27:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k-H4nsOTuxU&t=1647)
- Activation is the highest-leverage growth challenge in AI products because of "capability overhang" -- models improve faster than users update their habits; Amole advocates adding the right friction to onboarding (asking users questions to recommend the right product/feature) rather than cutting steps, a counterintuitive approach that has consistently improved conversion at Mercury, Masterclass, and Anthropic. [14:43](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k-H4nsOTuxU&t=883)

# Sam Altman’s Attack, Amazon vs. Starlink, and What Opus 4.7 Actually Means 

## Executive Summary

The video analyzes the rapid acceleration of AI capabilities, highlighting the release of Anthropic's Opus 4.7 and the shift from manual model tuning to prompt-based control.

# Elon Musk: Optimus 3 Is Coming, Recursive Self-Improvement Is Already Here, and the Singularity #239

## Executive Summary

Elon Musk joins Peter Diamandis on stage at the Abundance Summit for a wide-ranging conversation covering Optimus 3 production timelines, recursive self-improvement, the singularity, and universal high income. Musk confirms that recursive self-improvement is already underway with each successive AI model being built by its predecessor, and predicts fully automated AI self-improvement without human involvement by end of 2026 or early 2027. He declares we are currently "in the hard takeoff" and predicts the economy will 10x in 10 years, with money eventually becoming irrelevant as AI and robots produce goods and services that "saturate all human desire." Musk also announces Optimus 3 production beginning summer 2026 in a new 10-million-square-foot factory, with high-volume production by summer 2027.

## Key Points

- Musk states we are already in the "hard takeoff" of the singularity with recursive self-improvement well underway; fully automated AI self-improvement without humans in the loop may arrive by end of 2026, and he predicts the global economy will 10x within 10 years with a moon base and people on Mars.
- Optimus 3 production starts summer 2026 in a dedicated 10-million-square-foot factory, with Musk claiming it will be "by far the most advanced robot in the world" with nothing close; high-volume manufacturing is targeted for summer 2027, with a new improved robot design planned every year.
- Musk predicts universal high income will emerge through deflation as AI and robots produce goods and services far exceeding the money supply, eventually making money irrelevant entirely; he estimates an 80%+ probability of a great outcome, while acknowledging the singularity makes precise predictions impossible -- "it's called the singularity for a reason."

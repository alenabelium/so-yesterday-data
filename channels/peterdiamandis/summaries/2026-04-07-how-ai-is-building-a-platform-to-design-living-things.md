# How AI Is Building a Platform to Design Living Things 

## Executive Summary

Colossal is leveraging an AI-driven synthetic biology platform to solve complex biological challenges, ranging from de-extincting species like the woolly mammoth to creating microbes that degrade plastic. By treating every company as an AI entity, the organization has accelerated genetic editing capabilities from a few edits to hundreds with 90% efficiency, enabling a pipeline of spin-off companies. This approach transforms biology from a trial-and-error process into a precise engineering discipline capable of addressing global crises like invasive species and biodiversity loss.

## Key Points

- Adopt an 'AI-first' mindset in synthetic biology to scale genetic editing from single-point solutions to hundreds of simultaneous edits with 90% efficiency, drastically reducing development time [03:33](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Goa6c6Qz__I&t=213).
- Utilize a centralized R&D platform to spin out specialized companies for distinct problems, such as plastic degradation or disease-resistant crops, while sharing core AI and editing technologies across the ecosystem [03:33](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Goa6c6Qz__I&t=213).
- Address the $5.4 trillion invasive species market by developing humane gene drives that suppress populations without the ecological disruption caused by traditional poisoning or culling methods [25:41](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Goa6c6Qz__I&t=1541).
- Revolutionize IVF and embryo health by replacing archaic morphological grading with AI-driven, long-term observation models that identify the healthiest embryos more accurately [29:52](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Goa6c6Qz__I&t=1792)

Build or Be Replaced: The PM's New Job Description

Product management just split in two. Builders are thriving — record comp, more offers than ever, 14 out of 125 senior PMs becoming founders. Information movers face extinction. Companies will shed 30,000 to rehire 8,000 AI-first workers.

Three things every PM needs to understand right now:

📊 THE PM TRIAGE — A simple 2x2 that tells you which side you're on. Color-code your calendar for a week. Mostly yellow and red? You're in trouble.

🪞 THE SHADOW SUPERPOWER — The best performers are the most stuck. The better you mastered the old game, the harder it is to see the new one. Experienced developers are 19% slower with AI tools — while believing they're 24% faster.

✨ THE MOMENT OF JOY — What actually gets people through isn't fear or discipline. It's the moment you build something personal and feel the spark. Joy is the antidote to burnout.

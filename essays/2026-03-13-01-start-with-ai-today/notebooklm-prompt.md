Create an engaging video overview about getting started with AI as a working professional -- no jargon, no hype, just practical steps you can take today.

1. **The action gap is the real problem, not the knowledge gap.** 85% of Fortune 500 companies adopted Microsoft Copilot, but only 3% became paying users. The people getting real value push back with precision: "this section is too vague, give me specific numbers" or "rewrite the second paragraph in a more direct tone." The AI improves immediately. This is the habit that separates casual users from power users.

3. **The real risk is going slow, not going fast.** AI skills compound. The meta-skill -- specifying clearly, evaluating output, iterating toward better results -- gets stronger every week you practice. Every week you wait, the gap widens. You don't need to become an expert -- you need to become someone who uses AI the way you use email.

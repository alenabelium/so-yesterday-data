# The Intelligence Economy: How AI Is Rewriting the Business Model Playbook

Why the biggest business model shift since Uber is already here — and what companies must do to survive it.

🔥 The $285 billion SaaS sell-off proved it: when intelligence becomes a commodity, per-seat software becomes worthless. Klarna replaced Salesforce entirely. Sierra charges per resolution, not per seat. The model is inverting.

🏢 Five-person companies are generating $200M in revenue. AI-native teams run at 3-5x the output per employee. Meanwhile, your next customer might not even be human — McKinsey projects $900B+ in agent-mediated commerce by 2030.

🧭 MIT's four stages of business model transformation reveal where you stand — and most companies are stuck at Stage 1. The new scarce resource isn't intelligence. It's taste: knowing what to build and when to override the machine.

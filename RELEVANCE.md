# Relevance Criteria

Content on so-yesterday.ai must be about AI transformation, tools, strategy, or related topics. This document defines the criteria used to automatically classify whether a piece of content is relevant to the platform's mission.

## Relevant content includes:

- AI tool reviews, tutorials, and walkthroughs (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Codex, etc.)
- AI strategy, adoption, and organizational transformation
- LLM fundamentals: reasoning, context windows, memory, scaling, benchmarks
- AI agent architectures, agentic workflows, and autonomous systems
- Prompt engineering and context engineering techniques
- AI-related career advice and skill development
- AI ethics, safety, alignment, and regulation discussions
- Industry news: AI company announcements, releases, partnerships, acquisitions
- Coding and software development with AI assistance
- Productivity workflows enhanced by AI
- Analysis, commentary, and predictions about AI trends

## Irrelevant content includes:

- Music videos, vlogs, or entertainment unrelated to AI
- Product promotions or sponsored content not about AI tools
- Personal vlogs that don't discuss AI topics
- Gaming content (unless specifically about AI in gaming)
- Unrelated tech topics (phone reviews, hardware unboxing, etc.)
- Content shorter than 2 minutes (likely intros, teasers, or shorts)
- Cryptocurrency/blockchain content not related to AI
- General business advice with no AI connection
